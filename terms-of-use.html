Introduction
    Welcome to EnCars! We specialize in selling high-quality, pre-owned cars that undergo a 200+ point inspection to ensure the best standards for our customers. By purchasing a vehicle from EnCars, you agree to the following terms and conditions.
Eligibility Criteria for Purchase
    Priority Customers : We deal exclusively with company-serviced vehicles that have been regularly maintained by authorized service centers.
    First-Owner Cars Only : We only sell cars that have had a single previous owner, ensuring better longevity and minimal wear and tear.
    Native Regulation Cars Only : We sell vehicles that are registered in the same district to avoid cross-jurisdictional legal complications. We do not deal in out-of-district vehicles.
    Covered Parking Cars Only : All vehicles sold by EnCars have been stored in covered parking areas to protect them from environmental damage.
    Single-Hand Driven Cars Only : We offer cars that have been driven by a single owner to ensure consistency in driving habits and vehicle care.
    Professional User Cars Only : We cater to customers who treat their cars with care and value them like a family member, ensuring better vehicle condition.
    Good Water Used for Washing : All cars have been cleaned using high-quality, safe water to maintain the paint and body condition.
Vehicle Quality and Inspections
    200+ Point Inspection : Each car undergoes a rigorous 200+ point quality check to ensure its mechanical and structural integrity.
    No Accident or Flood-Affected Vehicles : EnCars does not deal in vehicles that have been involved in major accidents or have suffered flood damage.
    Complete Ownership History : Customers can access the full ownership and service history of the vehicle before making a purchase.
    Fair Pricing : Prices are set based on car condition, inspection reports, and market trends, ensuring transparency and value for money.
Purchase and Payment Terms
